Solet's home is beautiful -- a wonderful place for fairies to live. But a new human town has begun to be established nearby, and the peace of the fairies may soon be threatened...

Help Solet and her friends keep their home safe by dealing with the humans that get too close!




Read the story and solve one puzzle



Read the story and solve the puzzle while also trying to unlock as many hidden achievements as possible! After the game closes, you will be able to see how you did relative to other players.
